Searching for truths in the phygital era

Reality exists, independently of us. The truth matters. There are truths about reality, and we can try to find them. 
Even in an ageof multiple reality, I still believe in objective reality.
David J. Chalmers

The line between physical and digital, or virtual as some will put it, is slowly going obscured. Defining reality appears to be a rather difficult task when technology has become so advanced that virtual and simulated experiences are almost as "real" as our physical world. The essential question to be asked then is whether such demarcation between the physical and digital world can even be established, given the gradually befogged differences between the two worlds. 

Such question, I believe, will not be answered without the joint efforts of the dedicated researchers, thinkers and individuals who adhere to the thorough, logical and rigorous principles of scientific and humanistic research. However challenging the mission will be, we must keep on searching for objective reality in the era of radical relativism and alternative truths.
