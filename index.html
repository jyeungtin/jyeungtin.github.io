Justin W.N. Yeung

Staff Researcher of the Leiden Humanities AI and NLP group.

Hi there, my name is Justin Yeung!

🎓 I graduated BSc Communication Science from the University of Amsterdam and I am currently a MSc Social Data Science student at the Oxford Internet Institute, University of Oxford. I am currently working on graph robustness of criminal networks, supervised by Prof. Renaud Lambiotte.

🔬 My research interest mainly lies within (the subdomains of) network science, digital humanities and communication science.

🎙️ I am the podcast host of Voyage of Digitality.

🥊🎸🐱 Besides research and academic studies, I am a guitarist, a Muay Thai hobbyist and a cat dad.
