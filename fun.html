I am learning Julia!

Julia is an increasingly important tool in dynamical system modelling and agent-based modelling. It is a high-level language, but very efficient!

As an effort to supplement my skills in Python and R, I decided to take the opportunity to develop Julia as my third language for modelling complex systems.

I am now developing some easy codes to do some simulations on non-linear dynamics. You can find these in blogs.
