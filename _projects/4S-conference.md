## Project summary
In communication and journalism studies, digitality is always taken for granted without further interrogation of the conditions that make *digital media* possible. Not being able to recognize the materiality and transformativity of digitality across space and time means that we will fail to predict and address the challenges that come with them.

In this on-going project, [Dr. Tomás Dodds](https://tomasdodds.github.io/index.html) and I suggest a new framework towards understanding digitality with Process Theory. With this, we hope to shift our focus from *what is digitality* to *the becoming of digitality*.
